मातृभाषा दिवस (International Mother Language Day) CELEBRATION at
Indian Institute of Information Technology Ranchi Campus

IIIT Ranchi Celebrated the "मातृभाषा दिवस" this year on 21.02.2022 at its campus which included various music, poetry, speech, and dance performances with an elocution competition as the event highlight on the topic Origin and History of My Mother Tongue which saw many participants with multiple language including (Hindi, Telegu, Tamil, magni, Marwadi) where all participated expressed their love for their mother tongue.
